Alan Dhingra is a Canadian artist who has been painting for over 20 years. He is known for his contemporary style, which combines traditional techniques with modern themes. His work has been exhibited in gallery across Canada and internationally, and he has won numerous awards for his contributions to the art world. Dhingra's paintings often explore themes of identity, culture, and the human experience, making him a unique voice in the contemporary art scene.
